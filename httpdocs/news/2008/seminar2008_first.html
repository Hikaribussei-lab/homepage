発表者 叶茂(M2)
タイトル Electronic structure of functional materials: Heusler type magnetic shape memory alloy
要旨

The innovation of new material has always triggered the significant improvement of human history. However, the enhancement of the social productivity that benefits from the technology has caused the crisis of energy and environment. To solve this paradox, great interest and effort has been paid on the research work of new material that processes excellent functional properties with lower energy consumption. The so-called “smart material” is such a kind of functional materials that have received intensively research activities both in theoretical and experimental field, among which the Heusler type compounds are of great importance for the various novel properties.

In the family of Heusler type compounds, Ni2MnGa has been well known for its excellent magnetic shape memory effect for decades, while Co2MnGa is famous for showing large spin polarization at Fermi level. Effort has been paid to fabricate the mixed compound of these two alloys, with general formula of Ni2-xCoxMnGa, expecting to obtain novel materials with multi-functional properties. However, the temperature of martensitic phase transition in this system, which is closely related with the shape memory effect, decreases significantly as the concentration of Co increases. This system is stabilized at austenitic phase in the x>0.2 region.

In order to clarify the origin of martensitic phase transition as well as its stability against Co substitution from the view point of electronic structure, we performed high resolution photoemission spectroscopy (PES) with soft x-ray and hard x-ray synchrotron radiation.

The result shows clear modification of band structure by different Ni/Co ratios. And the samples with x?0.2 exhibit similar electronic structure change upon the occurrence of phase transition. The density of states (DOS) around binding energy of 0.3eV suddenly decreases in martensitic phase (30K). In the samples with x?0.2, the shifting of Ni 3d eg states, which is located at binding energy of 0.3eV and hybridized with 4p states in austenitic phase, to the higher binding energy should probably be responsible for the martensitic transition and stabilizes the martensitic phase in low temperature. However, the substitution of Co, with one less valence electron per atom, modifies the density of states near the Fermi level, and suppresses the stabilizing effect that induced by the shifting or splitting of Ni 3d eg states.

文献 [1] P. J. Webster et al., Philos. Mag. B 49, 295 (1984).
[2] S. R. Barman et al., PRB 72, 184410 (2005).

発表者 姜健 (M2)
タイトル Study of quasi-particle feature and many-body interaction of aluminum probed by high resolution angle-resolved photoemission
要旨

Recently, energy and momentum resolutions of ARPES has improved drastically. Now one can examine fine electronic structures of solids. Based on the analyses of spectral features in quantitative way, one can determine magnitudes of the electron-phonon and electron-electron interactions at any points on the Fermi surfaces. By examining quasi-particle properties in quantitative way, we will have more understanding on the origin of various physical properties of solids.

As a first step, I am examining a conventional superconductor Aluminum which is a textbook example of trivalent nearly-free electron metal. Although there have been many ARPES studies on Al, there are no report on the quasi-particle properties near the Fermi level. I consider detailed quantitative examinations of quasi-particles in Al will contribute to understand novel superconductors.

In this study, we have done high-resolution ARPES of Al(100) to clarify the surface- and bulk-derived Fermi surfaces, and to evaluate the many-body interactions on the quasi-particles near the Fermi level in the surface-derived state. The surface-derived band exhibits the "kink" structure in the energy dispersion at the energy of -40meV which coincides well with the Debye temperature ΘD= 426K (kΘD=37meV). It indicates that the structure is derived from the electron-phonon interaction. From the gradient of ReΣ, the electron-phonon coupling constant has been evaluated to be λ=0.6±0.05, which agrees reasonably well with the theoretical value 0.51.1)

発表者 田頭 徹朗 (M1)
タイトル Magnetism of small Fe clusters on Au(111) studied by x-ray magnetic circular dichroism
要旨 　表面再構成を起こすAu(111)上に蒸着されたFeは蒸着量の増加に伴い、fcc系でドット、ワイヤー、フィルムへと成長していく。P.OhresserらはX線内殻吸収磁気円二色性分光、磁気光学総和則を用いて、異なる次元でのFeの磁気特性を解析した。その結果、ドットからワイヤーに変化し始める膜厚(0.3ML)でスピン、軌道両磁気モーメントの値に大きな変化し、また同時に容易磁化方向も面内方向から面直方向への変化をも発見した。
　一般的に極薄膜厚での面内磁気異方性の例は類を見ない。これは極薄膜厚領域では電子の軌道が面内に制限されるため、軌道（つられてスピンの）磁気モーメントが面直方向を向きやすくなるからである。そのためこの変化は従来のスピン軌道相互作用だけでは説明がつかない。
　本光物性セミナーではこの異方性、磁気モーメントの変化をNeel model を用いて、格子のひずみ、格子間の磁気相互作用の観点から解明してく。

タイトル Electronic structure and magnetism of one-dimensional Fe monatomic wires on Au(788) investigated with ARPES and XMCD
要旨 　「1次元電子系の磁性」はパラメータが少なくシンプルであることから、２０世紀の初頭から理論的研究が行われている問題である。局在電子系の1次元イジング・モデルでは量子ゆらぎ、熱ゆらぎのために強磁性は発現しないことがわかっている。一方、1次元電子系における遍歴電子強磁性はLieb-Mattisの定理により禁止されている。しかし最近の理論研究によると、軌道縮退による厳密な1次元性の破れや、2原子鎖のような擬1次元系では強磁性が発現することが示唆されている。実験からのアプローチではW(110)、Cu(111)、Au(111)上のFeのような擬1次元構造の強磁性が観測されており、これらは2次元イジング・モデルで記述される。厳密な1次元系としてはPt(997)微傾斜面上のCo単原子鎖についての報告がある。
　本コロキウムでは、Au(788)微傾斜面上に作成したFe単原子鎖に対して角度分解光電子分光(ARPES)、X線内殻吸収磁気円二色性分光(XMCD分光)を行った研究について紹介する。ARPESによって強く局在したFe 3d電子が観測され、XMCD分光によって面直磁気異方性をもつ局在磁気モーメントがあることがわかった。またXMCDシグナルの温度、磁場依存性から、1次元イジング・モデルに期待される超常磁性が示唆された。この系における強磁性カップリングは直接交換相互作用とRKKY相互作用の競合の結果現れると推測している。
